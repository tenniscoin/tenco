Q4 2024 - Staking Program Release

We are excited to announce the release of the TennisCoin staking program! Users can lock their TennisCoins in the staking pool, hosted on PancakeSwap, to earn passive rewards while helping to secure and support the network. Stakers will receive rewards in the form of TennisCoin tokens, based on the amount staked and the duration of their commitment.

To join the staking pool on PancakeSwap:

  • Visit the PancakeSwap platform.
  • Navigate to the Pools section.
  • Search for TennisCoin or use this direct link to access our staking pool.

By participating in the PancakeSwap staking pool, holders can increase their earnings and contribute to the growth of the TennisCoin ecosystem.
